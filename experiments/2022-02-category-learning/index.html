How much time will it take and how much can you earn?

The study will take approx 100 minutes.
You will be paid £5 pounds for participation plus a bonus of up to £10 pounds depending on your performance.

Confidentiality

Your participation in this study will remain confidential. Your Prolific ID will NOT be shared with anyone outside the research team.

Participation and withdrawal

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you may refuse to participate
or you may choose to withdraw at any time.
However, you will only be paid for the participation upon completion
and if you enter the correct completion code provided at the end of the study.

Today, you find yourself in Monster World.
In Monster World, you are introduced to a new species of friendly monsters. All monsters differ from each other. However, the way they differ can be described in two dimensions:
  1. The spikiness of their head differs from not spiky to very spiky.
  2. The size of their belly varies from empty to full

Instructions

Your session today will be split up into three parts.

  1. Memory: Remember the spikiness of the monster's head and the fill of the monster's belly. You are only presented with a monster only for a short period of time on every trial. After that, you are required to adjust two sliders to reconstruct the head and the belly of the monster. Takes approx. 20 min
  2. Categorization: The monsters all look somewhat similar, but they come from different tribes. Use the information about the spikiness of their head and the fill of their belly to gauge what tribes they are from. You are going to get a feedback after every trial, which may help getting you started. Takes approx. 50 min. There will be breaks in between these 50 mins.
  3. Memory: The third part will be the same as the first part. Try to remember the monsters presented on every trial. Takes approx. 20 min

Part two of the experiment

In the second part, your task is to categorize the monsters into c categories. You can use the number keys on your keyboard to give responses in the task. After every response you are given feedback whether your response was correct or not. In the latter case the category that would have been true is going to be displayed to you.
